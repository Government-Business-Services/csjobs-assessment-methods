What elements do you want to assess?

Select what elements you want to assess candidates on at sift and interview.

Success profiles are characteristics broken down into 5 elements to help you find the right candidate for your job role.

You will create a 'success profile' for your job role using the 5 elements to assess the job role's requirements.

Each element can be assessed in a number of ways to suit the nature of the vacancy and used at any stage of the assessment process. Not all of the elements are relevant to every role, and will vary depending on the profession, level and type of role. Vacancy holders can decide what elements are most important to assess based on the job role requirements.

If you would like to learn more about how to assess candidates, you can sign up for Success Profile training.
